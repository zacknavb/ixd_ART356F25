Records of The Drakith War

By Zachary Baker

Description

Hello, welcome to my game! This game is heavily inspired by Vampire Survivors and similar games and is my take on the genre fully using p5js and p5play.

If you have never played Vampire Survivors or any other bullet heaven, you play by automatically attacking in the direction you're facing and defeating swarms of enemies.

Enemies drop experience for you to collect which helps you level up! Each level allows you to upgrade any specific stat.

Get stronger and defeat hordes of enemies until you either succumb to their numbers or survive till time runs out and claim victory!

Have Fun!


Controls

Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys

Pause: Escape Key


Debugging Controls

Turn on hitboxes: 1

Turn off hitboxes: 2
